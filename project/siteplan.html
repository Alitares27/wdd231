Site Plan

Site Name:

NestBook Store

Site Purpose:

The website serves as an online bookstore that allows visitors to explore and purchase a wide variety of books. It provides detailed information on different book categories, recommendations for readers, and special promotions.

Scenarios:

Which books are best for beginners who want to start reading fiction?

A visitor is looking for recommendations on books that are easy to read and engaging for someone new to reading fiction.

How can I find discounted books or special promotions on the website?

A customer wants to know if there are ongoing discounts, seasonal promotions, or a section for affordable books.

Do you offer digital books or only physical copies?

A visitor is interested in knowing if they can buy e-books in addition to printed books.
